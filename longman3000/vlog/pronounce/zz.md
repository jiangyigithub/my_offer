1. p --> b

2. t --> d
   establish
3. k --> g
   sky

## 连读规则
1. 单词末尾辅音 + 单词开头元音
(连读时要把前面的音往后移)
like a --> li ka
check in --> che kin
hold it --> hol dit
sleep in --> slee pin
like it --> li kit

2. 如果单词末尾的辅音是清辅音的话，连读时要稍微浊化
(t->d)
What are you doing?
get it --> ge dit
a lot of --> a lodof
pick it up --> pic ki dup

3. 带辅音`n`的连读
can I
in an hour

4. 带辅音`l`的连读
all of
all out of

5. 口头缩写
want to -->wanna
